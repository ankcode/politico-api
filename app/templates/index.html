/api/v1/parties/<party-id>/name
 Edit Party name
DELETE 
/api/v1/parties/<party-id>
 Delete Party

/api/v1/offices
 Create new office
GET 
/api/v1/offices
 Fetch all offices

/api/v1/offices/<office-id>
